Increase your value and build your network... close to home.

The UCF Evening MBA Program is designed for the working professional to achieve his or her academic and professional goals. Evening classes are led by the UCF College of Business Administration's world-class faculty members and are comprised of professionals from many of Central Florida's leading employers.

In addition...
  • The UCF Evening MBA may be able to waive the GMAT requirement for you based on other criteria for admission.
  • You may also qualify for an exemption of the two-year post baccalaureate work requirement based on employment before or during your undergraduate education.
